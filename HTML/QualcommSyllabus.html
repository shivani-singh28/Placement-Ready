Qualcomm Aptitude Test Syllabus 2022

The Qualcomm Aptitude Test Syllabus 2022 the total time of the test is 90 mins and in this time you have to solve a total of 60 questions, which contain quants, programming MCQ compulsory for both CS/IT and Electrical streams. However for CS/IT there is Technical section on Computer Science and for electrical branches they have Electronics Technical Test.


Qualcomm Online Test Pattern

  • Total Number of Questions – 60 Questions
  • Total Time – 90 mins
  • Quants Questions

  • Programming MCQ Questions
