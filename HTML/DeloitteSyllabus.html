Deloitte Online Test Pattern 2023


Basically, there are 4 sections in the test of Deloitte written test pattern


  • Quantitative Aptitude and Logical Reasoning
  • English
  • Computer Fundamentals
  • Coding
  • Versant Test(it may get asked as it vary from college to college)

Deloitte Written Test Syllabus 2023 Order of Sections


There is no particular order of the section in the exam but your ranks in the section were considered in a very significant manner by this order– first is Quants and Logical Reasoning then, Verbal English followed by Computer Fundamentals and finally Coding Round.
  • You can Swap between Questions in a section i.e. next and previous options are available to go to the next question or previous question
  • You can’t swap between Sections while doing Quants. You can’t go to English unless the time is over or you submit.
