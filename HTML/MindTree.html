MindTree Communication Assessment Detailed Information 2023:

  • Section A- Speaking( You will see few sentences on your desktop screen, you will have to read and repeat those sentences).
  • Section B- Speaking & Listening(Listen the audio carefully and repeat the sentences).
  • Section C- Fill in the blank with correct word(An audio will be played in which you have to fill in the blank space part to complete the sentence)
  • Section D- Correct the sentences (An audio will be played and you will have to correct that audio
  • Section E- You will be given scenarios in an audio format and then questions will be asked.
  • Section F- Incomplete conversation between two person will play, listen to the conversation and then complete the sentences.
    • \
  • Section G- A topic will be given to you and you will have to speak for 1 minute on the topic.

Important Information:


  • Total Number of Questions: 25-30
  • Total Time:20 Mins
  • Difficulty:High
  • Importance:High
